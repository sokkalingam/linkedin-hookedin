## üéØ DELIVERY TIPS

## üé¨ SHOOTING SCRIPT (What to Actually Do)

### Take 1: Camera Introduction (Record this first)
1. Set up your camera
2. Do a test recording (30 seconds)
3. Check lighting, audio, framing
4. When ready, slate it: "HookedIn Presentation, Take 1"
5. Smile, take a breath
6. Deliver **PART 1: Hook + Problem**
7. Pause 2 seconds
8. Deliver **PART 2: Solution**
9. Say "Cut!" and stop recording

**Tips:**
- If you mess up, don't stop! Pause, and restart that sentence
- You can edit out mistakes later
- Do 2-3 takes and pick the best one

---

### Take 2: Screen Recording (Record this second)
1. Open HookedIn in browser
2. Open LinkedIn Developer Portal in another tab
3. Start screen recording
4. Wait 3 seconds
5. Navigate to HookedIn and perform the demo
6. Go slowly and deliberately
7. End with 3 seconds of the deployment instructions on screen
8. Stop recording

**Tips:**
- Don't narrate during recording (add voiceover later)
- If you mess up, just restart from that step
- Keep mouse movements smooth

---

### Take 3: Voiceover for Demo (Record audio only)
1. Import your screen recording into editing software
2. Watch it through once
3. Open voice recording app or use video editor
4. Record **PART 3: Demo** voiceover while watching the video
5. Match your narration timing to the actions on screen

**Tips:**
- Pause recording when the screen is loading
- It's okay to record in segments
- You can record each screen separately then edit together

---

### Take 4: Camera Closing (Record this last)
1. Same setup as Take 1
2. Slate it: "HookedIn Closing, Take 1"
3. Deliver **PART 4: Impact + Close**
4. Hold your final pose for 3 seconds (for editing)
5. Say "Cut!" and stop
